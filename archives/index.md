# Ateliers web archives

Publication space for _web design_ and _creative coding_ projects by students at the École supérieure d'art et de design des Pyrénées.

The workshop's projects and technical and cultural resources have been migrated to [radicalweb.design](https://radicalweb.design).

This space is made functional by a program developed by [Erwan Demay](https://github.com/ErwanDemay) and [Julien Bidoret](https://accentgrave.net), released under MIT license: [github.com/esadpyrenees/archiver](https://github.com/esadpyrenees/archiver). It uses the [Parsedown](https://github.com/erusev/parsedown), [ParsedownExtra](https://github.com/erusev/parsedown-extra) and [ParsedownExtraPlugin](https://github.com/taufik-nurrohman/parsedown-extra-plugin/) libraries.
